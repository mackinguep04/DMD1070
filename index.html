Mansfield Supply

Real hardware solutions from real people.

You don't have to fend for yourself. Let us give you the right tools for the job and the know-how to use them.

Mansfield Supply is a small hardware store in Storrs, CT. For over 50 years we've been dedicated to serving customers at a level that exceeds expectations. That's the joy of family business: we care about our work, and we care about you.

Here you can find a little bit of everything, from power tools to piping to that tiny screw that just disappeared. And it's not just the service--our customers come here for quality products at the best prices around. If we don't have it, we'll find it. Feel free to browse, give us a call, or just stop by! You'll be glad you did.
